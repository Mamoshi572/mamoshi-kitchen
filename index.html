Our Story

From street vendor to Nairobi's favorite food spot

Family Owned Since 2020

Bringing Authentic Kenyan Flavors to Your Doorstep

Ashen Bites began as a small street food stall in Githurai, driven by a passion for sharing authentic Kenyan cuisine. What started with just two recipes has grown into Nairobi's most loved street food delivery service.

We use traditional recipes passed down through generations, combined with modern food safety standards to deliver the perfect balance of authenticity and quality.

Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you deliver to?

We deliver to all areas within Nairobi and its environs. Delivery time varies based on location but typically takes 30-45 minutes.

How do I pay with M-Pesa?

Select M-Pesa at checkout, enter your phone number, and you'll receive a prompt to enter your M-Pesa PIN on your phone.

Do you offer discounts?

Yes! We have weekly specials and discounts for large orders. Check our menu for current promotions.
